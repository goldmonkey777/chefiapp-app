# Privacy Policy - ChefIApp

**Last Updated:** November 29, 2024

## 1. Introduction

ChefIApp ("we", "our" or "company") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data of our ChefIApp mobile application users (the "App").

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Data Provided Directly by You
- **Full name**
- **Email address**
- **Professional profile information** (position, department, company)
- **Profile photo** (optional)
- **Authentication data** (login credentials via Google OAuth)

### 2.2 Automatically Generated Data
- **Performance data** (XP scores, rankings, completed tasks)
- **Usage data** (App interactions, features used)
- **Technical data** (device type, operating system, App version)
- **System logs** (error logs and activities for maintenance)

### 2.3 Third-Party Data
- **Google OAuth:** Name, email, and profile picture from your Google account
- **Supabase:** Secure storage of user data and authentication

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:
- Create and manage your user account
- Provide App features (task management, rankings, XP system)
- Improve user experience and personalize content
- Communicate updates, notifications, and technical support
- Analyze usage patterns for App improvements
- Ensure security and prevent fraud
- Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on:
- **Consent:** By creating an account and accepting this policy
- **Contract performance:** To provide requested services
- **Legitimate interest:** To improve the App and ensure security
- **Legal obligation:** To comply with applicable legal requirements

## 5. Data Sharing

### 5.1 We Don't Sell Your Data
We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

### 5.2 Limited Sharing
We may share data with:
- **Third-party service providers:**
  - Supabase (data storage and authentication)
  - Google (OAuth authentication)
  - Google Gemini AI (analysis processing - anonymized data)
- **Your company managers:** Performance data visible to your supervisors
- **Legal authorities:** When required by law or court order

## 6. Storage and Security

### 6.1 Data Location
Your data is stored on secure Supabase servers located in the European Union (as configured).

### 6.2 Security Measures
- Data encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Data encryption at rest
- Multi-factor authentication for administrative access
- Regular backups and recovery systems
- Continuous security monitoring

### 6.3 Retention Period
- **Active account data:** Retained while account is active
- **Data after account deletion:** 30 days (to allow recovery)
- **Legal/tax data:** Up to 7 years (when required by law)
- **Anonymized data:** May be retained indefinitely for analytics

## 7. Your Rights (GDPR and LGPD)

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Erasure:** Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Portability:** Receive your data in structured format
- **Object:** Object to the processing of your data
- **Restriction:** Restrict processing under certain circumstances
- **Withdraw consent:** Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@chefiapp.com

## 8. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The App uses:
- **Session cookies:** To maintain active session
- **Local storage:** For data caching and better performance
- **Analytics:** To understand how the App is used (anonymized data)

You can manage these preferences in device settings.

## 9. International Data Transfers

If we transfer data outside the EU/EEA, we ensure adequate protections through:
- Standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission
- Recognized privacy certifications
- Explicit consent when necessary

## 10. Protection of Minors

The App is not intended for individuals under 16 years of age. We do not intentionally collect data from minors. If you become aware that a minor has provided data, contact us for immediate deletion.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes through:
- In-App notification
- Email to registered users
- Update of the "Last Updated" date at the top
